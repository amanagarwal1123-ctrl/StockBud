    *   A Physical vs. Book page for stamp-wise verification (quick gross weight check) and detailed item-wise comparison (via file upload).
    *   Date pickers for all relevant analytics and upload pages.
    *   CSV export functionality for all data tables.
    *   Indian currency formatting (Lakh/Crore) for all monetary values.
